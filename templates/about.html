lyrX

About

lyrX is a free, easy to use song lyric browser. Its made to make lyrics accurate and accessible

Warning: lyrX is in open beta. Features may be unstable and/or change.

How you can help

Request lyrics

You can request song lyrics through a form in our Discord server.

Report invalid lyrics

Because most lyrics are sourced from public websites, there may be invalid, wrong ones.

You can use the Report button on the track page to report these invalid lyrics
